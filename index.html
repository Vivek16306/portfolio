Internship work sample

SSW IT Student Worker Binder

During my internship at the Rutgers School of Social Work, I served as IT Support providing technical assistance to faculty and staff across the department. My responsibilities were both hands-on and service-oriented, giving me exposure to a wide range of real-world IT tasks.

One of my primary duties was computer imaging — deploying a standardized operating system and software configuration across multiple machines using WDS/MDT. I also performed hardware setup and configuration, maintained an accurate IT inventory, and provided remote troubleshooting for staff using remote desktop tools.

My most impactful project was the creation of the SSW IT Student Worker Binder — a comprehensive 20-page documentation resource covering 13 IT services with step-by-step procedures, escalation protocols, and onboarding checklists. I developed this proactively so any student worker could reference it when senior staff were unavailable — demonstrating my ability to think beyond day-to-day tasks and contribute to long-term operational efficiency.
