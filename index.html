SSW IT Student Worker Binder

I've worked in IT Support at the Rutgers School of Social Work since November 2025, and this summer that role is being formalized as an internship for my degree. My responsibilities are both hands-on and service-oriented, giving me exposure to a wide range of real-world IT tasks.

One of my primary duties is computer imaging — deploying a standardized operating system and software configuration across multiple machines using WDS/MDT. I also handle hardware setup and configuration, maintain an accurate IT inventory, and provide remote troubleshooting for staff using remote desktop tools.

My most impactful project so far has been creating the SSW IT Student Worker Binder — a comprehensive 20-page documentation resource covering 13 IT services with step-by-step procedures, escalation protocols, and onboarding checklists. I developed this proactively so any student worker could reference it when senior staff were unavailable — demonstrating my ability to think beyond day-to-day tasks and contribute to long-term operational efficiency.
